 A flood is a terrifying scene, sometimes men and women can be seen crying for help. During a flood, the government have to take immediate reliefs and rescue measures and sanction huge funds for the purpose. To alleviate the suffering of the victims of flood, relief camps are open where people People are provided with shelter, food, clothing, medicine and others, but prevention is better than cure. So now different organizations are taking part of this by creating camps and educating people on how to act on a flood event.
